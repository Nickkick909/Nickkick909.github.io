Dodge Car was my project for winter break 2018.
This is a game I created using C# and Unity.
You are a little car on a long platform, and you have to dodge obstacles.
If you make it to the end of the level you get to move on.
There are 10 hand built levels for you to navigate.
The levels increase in difficultly as you progress.
I also made an installer for Windows and Mac.
