The Pacman Projects are from my Artificial Intelligence class at UofSC.
These projects were to help Pacman navigate his maze world.
The first project was a searching problem to help him find the closest food.
The next one involved Pacman finding food while avoiding ghosts.
Third project was about reinforcement learning.
Pacman was given several tries, and each time he improved and updated his knowledge.
